What is Salman Khan's Biography?

Salman Khan was born to a prominent film family in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. His father, Salim Khan, is a renowned screenwriter, while his mother, Sushila Charak, is a homemaker. Salman Khan has two brothers, Arbaaz and Sohail, and two sisters, Alvira and Arpita. He made his acting debut in 1988 with the film "Biwi-O-Biwi" but gained recognition for his role in "Maine Pyar Kiya" (1989). Since then, he has starred in numerous successful films, establishing himself as one of Bollywood's leading actors.

Zanjeer, or traditional chains, holds a special place in Indian culture, often worn by brides during weddings as a symbol of their marital status. Apart from their aesthetic appeal, zanjeer also represents a bond of love and commitment between partners. The significance of zanjeer includes:

  • Symbolism: Represents the bond of marriage and commitment between partners.
  • Cultural Heritage: Reflects the rich traditions and customs associated with Indian weddings.
  • Fashion Statement: Adds elegance and style to bridal attire, making it a cherished accessory.

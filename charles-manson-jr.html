Who is Charles Manson Jr.?

Charles Manson Jr. is often defined by his father's notorious history. Born on April 12, 1966, in Los Angeles, California, he is the son of Charles Manson and his then-girlfriend, Mary Brunner. Growing up, he faced immense challenges due to the stigma attached to his family's name. His childhood was filled with turmoil, as he was often confronted with the legacy of terror that his father had created.

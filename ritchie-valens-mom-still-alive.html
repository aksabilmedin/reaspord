Who Was Ritchie Valens? A Brief Biography

Ritchie Valens was born on May 13, 1941, in Pacoima, California. He was the son of Mexican immigrant parents, Concha and Joseph Valenzuela. Ritchie showed an early interest in music and began playing the guitar at a young age. His musical career began to take off when he was discovered by Bob Keane, the owner of Del-Fi Records. Ritchie Valens quickly gained popularity, becoming one of the first Latino rock stars.

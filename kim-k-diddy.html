Who is Kim Kardashian?

Kim Kardashian is a prominent media personality, socialite, and entrepreneur. Born on October 21, 1980, in Los Angeles, California, she rose to fame with the reality television show "Keeping Up with the Kardashians," which aired from 2007 to 2021. Kim has since built a successful brand, including her beauty line KKW Beauty and her shapewear line SKIMS.

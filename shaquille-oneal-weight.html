What is Shaquille O'Neal's Biography?

Shaquille O'Neal, often referred to as "Shaq," was born on March 6, 1972, in Newark, New Jersey. He displayed remarkable athletic skills from a young age, excelling in basketball and other sports. After a stellar college career at LSU, Shaquille was selected as the first overall pick in the 1992 NBA Draft by the Orlando Magic. Over his 19-year career, he played for several teams, including the Los Angeles Lakers, Miami Heat, Phoenix Suns, Cleveland Cavaliers, and Boston Celtics. Shaquille is a four-time NBA champion and a 15-time All-Star, known for his dominant style of play and charismatic personality.

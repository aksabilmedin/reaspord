What is the Biography of Princesa Diana?

Princesa Diana, born Diana Frances Spencer on July 1, 1961, in Sandringham, Norfolk, was an iconic figure whose life story continues to resonate with people around the world. She became the Princess of Wales upon her marriage to Prince Charles in 1981 and captured the hearts of millions through her charitable work and humanitarian efforts. Tragically, her life was cut short in a car accident in Paris on August 31, 1997, leaving behind a legacy that transcends her royal duties.

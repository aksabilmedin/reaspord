What is Tara Westover's Biography?

Tara Westover was born on September 27, 1986, in Clifton, Idaho, to parents who were survivalists and mistrusted formal education. Her upbringing was marked by a lack of traditional schooling, as her family preferred to educate themselves outside of conventional systems. Despite these challenges, Tara's determination to learn led her to pursue higher education, eventually earning her a PhD from the University of Cambridge. Her memoir "Educated," published in 2018, has received widespread acclaim and has been translated into numerous languages.

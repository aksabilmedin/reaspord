What is the Biography of Tony Woodcock?

Tony Woodcock was born on December 23, 1983, in Auckland, New Zealand. He began playing rugby at a young age and quickly showed promise as a talented front-row forward. His dedication to the sport led him to join the prestigious Auckland rugby team, where he honed his skills and gained invaluable experience. Woodcock's career took off when he was selected to represent New Zealand in international matches, where he quickly became known for his powerful scrummaging and agility on the field.

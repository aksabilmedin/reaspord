What is the Biography of Jennifer Lopez?

Jennifer Lopez was born on July 24, 1969, in The Bronx, New York, to Puerto Rican parents. She grew up in a modest household, where her love for performing arts began at an early age. After studying at Baruch College, she pursued a career in dance, landing roles in various music videos and television shows. Her breakthrough came in the late 1990s with her role in the biopic "Selena," which catapulted her to stardom.

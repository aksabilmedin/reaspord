Who Was Princess Diana Gore? A Brief Biography

Princess Diana Gore, born Diana Frances Spencer on July 1, 1961, in Sandringham, Norfolk, England, quickly captured the world’s attention when she became engaged to Prince Charles in 1981. A member of the British royal family, she was known for her charitable work and her ability to connect with people from all walks of life. Diana's marriage to Prince Charles was highly publicized and often tumultuous, leading to their eventual separation in 1992 and divorce in 1996.

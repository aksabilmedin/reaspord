What is Gerard Butler's Biography?

Gerard Butler was born on November 13, 1969, in Glasgow, Scotland. He grew up in a close-knit family with a passion for performing arts from a young age. After studying law at the University of Glasgow, he decided to pursue a career in acting. His breakthrough came in the early 2000s, leading to a series of successful films that established him as a leading man in Hollywood.

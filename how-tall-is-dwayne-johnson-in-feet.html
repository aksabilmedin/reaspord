What is Dwayne Johnson's Biography?

Dwayne Douglas Johnson was born on May 2, 1972, in Hayward, California. He comes from a family with a rich history in professional wrestling; his father, Rocky Johnson, and grandfather, Peter Maivia, were both wrestlers. Dwayne himself began his career in the WWE, where he quickly rose to fame and became one of the most popular faces in wrestling history. His transition to acting was seamless, as he brought his charisma and physicality to the big screen, starring in blockbuster hits.

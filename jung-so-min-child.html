What is the Biography of Jung So-min?

Jung So-min was born on March 16, 1989, in Seoul, South Korea. She developed an interest in acting at a young age and later pursued a degree in Theater and Film from the Seoul National University. Her breakthrough came when she starred in the television drama "Playful Kiss," which garnered her widespread recognition. Over the years, she has appeared in numerous dramas and films, showcasing her versatility as an actress.

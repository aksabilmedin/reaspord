Who is Lee Sung-Kyung?

Lee Sung-Kyung is a South Korean actress and model, born on August 10, 1990, in Gyeongju, South Korea. She gained fame after participating in the reality television program "Korea’s Next Top Model" in 2012, where she secured first place. Her modeling career paved the way for her transition into acting, where she showcased her versatility and charm. Over the years, Lee has starred in several hit dramas, including "Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo," "Cheese in the Trap," and "The Beauty Inside."

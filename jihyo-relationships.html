What is Jihyo's Biography?

Jihyo, whose real name is Park Ji-soo, was born on February 1, 1997, in Jeju, South Korea. She has been a prominent figure in the K-Pop industry since her debut with TWICE in 2015. As the leader of the group, she has played a crucial role in its success, leading her fellow members through various challenges and triumphs. Her powerful vocals and captivating stage presence have garnered her a loyal fanbase, making her one of the most recognizable faces in K-Pop.

What is Salman Khan's Biography?

Salman Khan is one of the most celebrated actors in Indian cinema, known for his charisma and immense fan following. Born on December 27, 1965, in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, he hails from a family with a rich legacy in the film industry. His father, Salim Khan, is a renowned screenwriter, while his siblings include actors Arbaaz Khan and Sohail Khan. Salman made his acting debut in 1988 with the film “Biwi-O-Biwi” but gained widespread fame with “Maine Pyar Kiya” in 1989.

Over the years, Salman has starred in numerous hit films, including “Baajrangi Bhaijaan,” “Sultan,” and “Kick,” establishing himself as a box office powerhouse. Beyond acting, he is also a producer, television presenter, and philanthropist, known for his charitable organization, Being Human. Despite his success, Salman Khan's personal life has often made headlines, particularly his relationships and speculations about marriage.

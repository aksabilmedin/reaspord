What happened during Lina Medina's pregnancy?

Lina's story is both fascinating and unsettling. At just 5 years old, she was brought to a hospital by her parents due to abdominal swelling. After medical examinations, it was confirmed that she was approximately 7 months pregnant. The news shocked the medical community and the world. Lina gave birth to a healthy baby boy via cesarean section, as her pelvis was too small for a natural delivery.

How did Lina become pregnant at such a young age?

The circumstances surrounding Lina's pregnancy have been the subject of speculation and controversy. It is believed that she experienced precocious puberty, a rare condition that causes early sexual development. Medical professionals determined that her body had developed to a stage where she could conceive, despite her young age. However, the question of how this could happen remains a sensitive topic, as it involves allegations of sexual abuse, which were never conclusively proven.

Who is James Harden? A Brief Biography

James Edward Harden Jr. was born on August 26, 1989, in Los Angeles, California. He emerged as a basketball prodigy during his high school years at Artesia High School, where he led his team to a state championship. Harden continued to shine at Arizona State University, earning accolades and a spot in the NBA draft. He was selected third overall by the Oklahoma City Thunder in 2009.

Harden's career took off when he was traded to the Houston Rockets in 2012, where he became a perennial All-Star and one of the league's top scorers. His unique playing style, which combines powerful drives to the basket with an exceptional shooting touch, has redefined modern basketball. Off the court, Harden is known for his philanthropy and business ventures, making him a well-rounded public figure.

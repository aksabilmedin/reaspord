What is Daniel Day-Lewis's Biography?

Daniel Day-Lewis was born on April 29, 1957, in London, England. He hails from a family of artists; his father, Cecil Day-Lewis, was a poet, and his mother, Jill Balcon, was an actress. This creative upbringing laid the foundation for his eventual success in the film industry. Day-Lewis attended the prestigious Bristol Old Vic Theatre School, which helped him hone his craft and prepare for a career that would see him become one of the most respected actors of his generation.

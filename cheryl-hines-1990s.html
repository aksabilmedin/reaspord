What is Cheryl Hines' Biography?

Cheryl Hines was born on September 21, 1965, in Miami Beach, Florida. She grew up in Tallahassee, where her love for performing arts blossomed at a young age. After completing her education, Hines moved to Los Angeles to pursue her dreams in acting and comedy.

Cheryl Hines began her career in the late 1990s, drawing inspiration from her experiences in improv and sketch comedy. What Impact Did 'Curb Your Enthusiasm' Have on Cheryl Hines' Career?

Although "Curb Your Enthusiasm" debuted in 2000, the groundwork for Hines' success was laid during the late 1990s.

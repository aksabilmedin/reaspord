What is Colin Morgan's Biography?

Colin Morgan was born on January 1, 1986, in Belfast, Northern Ireland. He attended the Royal Irish Academy of Music and later pursued his acting studies at the Drama School in Cardiff. His professional career began with various stage performances, but it was his role in "Merlin" that truly launched him into the limelight. Over the years, Colin has taken on various challenging roles, showcasing his range and depth as an actor.

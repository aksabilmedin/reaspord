What is the Background of Yuji Itadori?

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. A high school student with exceptional physical abilities, Yuji's life takes a drastic turn when he becomes involved in the world of curses and sorcery. After consuming a cursed object, he becomes the host for Ryomen Sukuna, a powerful curse. Despite the challenges he faces, Yuji remains committed to saving others and fighting against the forces of evil.

What About Nobara Kugisaki's Character?

Nobara Kugisaki is introduced as a strong-willed and confident sorcerer with a fierce attitude. She hails from a rural area and possesses unique abilities that allow her to combat curses effectively. Nobara's character is defined by her determination, resilience, and unwavering loyalty to her friends. Her dynamic personality complements Yuji's, making them a formidable team in the battle against curses.

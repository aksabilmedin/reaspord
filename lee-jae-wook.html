What is the Biography of Lee Jae-wook?

Lee Jae-wook was born on May 10, 1998, in the vibrant city of Seoul, South Korea. He graduated from the prestigious Sungkyunkwan University, where he majored in the Department of Theater and Film. How Did Lee Jae-wook Start His Career?

Lee Jae-wook's career took off in 2018 when he made his official acting debut in the popular web drama "A-TEEN." His natural talent and magnetic presence quickly caught the attention of viewers and industry professionals alike. Following this success, he landed roles in various dramas, showcasing his versatility as an actor.

His breakthrough role came in the historical drama "The Tale of Nokdu," where he played the character of Jeon Nok-du. The series was well-received, and Lee's performance was praised for its depth and authenticity. This pivotal moment solidified his status as a rising star in the entertainment industry.

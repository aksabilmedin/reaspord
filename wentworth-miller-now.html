Who is Wentworth Miller? A Brief Biography

Wentworth Miller was born on June 2, 1972, in Chipping Norton, Oxfordshire, England, to an American father and a British mother. He moved to the United States at a young age, and his upbringing in a multicultural environment shaped his worldview and artistic expression. Miller attended Princeton University, where he earned a degree in English literature, which later influenced his work as a writer and actor.

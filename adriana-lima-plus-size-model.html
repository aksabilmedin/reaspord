Who is Adriana Lima?

Adriana Lima is a Brazilian supermodel, born on June 12, 1981, in Salvador, Bahia. She gained global fame as one of the most recognized Victoria's Secret Angels, holding the title for nearly two decades. Lima's striking features, including her captivating blue-green eyes and full lips, have made her a favorite in the fashion world. Her career began at a young age when she won a modeling contest, which ultimately led to her signing with Elite Model Management in New York City.

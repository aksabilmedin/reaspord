What is Emory Cohen's Biography?

Emory Cohen was born on March 13, 1990, in Woodmere, New York. He grew up in a family that encouraged creativity and self-expression, which played a significant role in shaping his passion for acting. After attending the renowned Long Island High School for the Arts, he pursued further training at the prestigious Stella Adler Studio of Acting in New York City. His early experiences in theater provided him with a solid foundation, leading to his breakout role in the critically acclaimed film "The Place Beyond the Pines" (2012).

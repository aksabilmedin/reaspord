How Did Jerry Yan Become Famous?

Jerry Yan's fame skyrocketed after his role in "Meteor Garden," the adaptation of the Japanese manga "Boys Over Flowers." The series, which aired in 2001, was a massive hit across Asia and made Jerry a household name. His portrayal of Dao Ming Si, the leader of the F4 group, resonated with audiences, turning him into a teen idol almost overnight.

Throughout his career, Jerry has achieved numerous milestones that highlight his versatility and dedication to his craft. Some of his most notable works include:

  • Chinese Paladin 3 (2009) - A fantasy drama that expanded his fan base in mainland China.

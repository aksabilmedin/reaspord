What is the Background of Yuji and Nobara?

Yuji Itadori is the protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen, born with extraordinary physical abilities that allow him to excel in athletics. After a series of unexpected events, he becomes the host for Sukuna, a powerful curse, which sets him on a path as a jujutsu sorcerer. His moral compass and unwavering determination to protect others define his character.

Nobara Kugisaki, on the other hand, hails from a rural town in Japan. Her strong-willed personality and fierce independence make her a force to be reckoned with. Armed with her unique techniques involving cursed tools, Nobara proves her worth as a jujutsu sorcerer and stands as a role model for those who value strength and resilience.

What Are Yuji and Nobara’s Unique Abilities?

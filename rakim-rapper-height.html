What is Rakim's Biography?

Rakim, born William Michael Griffin Jr. on January 28, 1968, in Wyandanch, New York, is known for his profound influence on hip-hop music. He gained prominence in the late 1980s as part of the duo Eric B. & Rakim. The pair released several groundbreaking albums, including "Paid in Full," which is often cited as one of the greatest hip-hop albums of all time. Rakim's intricate lyricism and innovative flow set the standard for future generations of rappers.

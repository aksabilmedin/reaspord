What is Tom Cruise's Biography?

Tom Cruise, born Thomas Cruise Mapother IV on July 3, 1962, in Syracuse, New York, is one of Hollywood's most recognizable and successful actors. He began his acting career in the early 1980s and quickly rose to fame with his roles in films such as "Risky Business" (1983), "Top Gun" (1986), and "Jerry Maguire" (1996). Over the years, Cruise has received numerous accolades, including three Golden Globe Awards and three Academy Award nominations.

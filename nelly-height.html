Who is Nelly? A Brief Biography

Nelly, born Cornell Iral Haynes Jr. on November 2, 1974, in Austin, Texas, is an iconic figure in the music industry. He rose to fame in the early 2000s with his debut album "Country Grammar," which included hit singles like "Hot in Herre" and "Ride Wit Me." Nelly's music often blends elements of hip-hop, pop, and R&B, making him a versatile artist who appeals to a wide audience. Over the years, he has released several successful albums, won multiple awards, and even ventured into acting and business.

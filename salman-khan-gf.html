What is Salman Khan's Biography?

Salman Khan, born on December 27, 1965, in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, is one of the most celebrated actors in Bollywood. He made his acting debut in 1988 with the film "Biwi-O-Biwi," but it was the 1989 blockbuster "Maine Pyar Kiya" that catapulted him to stardom. Over the years, he has starred in numerous hit films, including "Baazigar," "Karan Arjun," "Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!", "Bajrangi Bhaijaan," and "Sultan." Apart from his acting career, Salman is also a producer and television presenter, known for hosting the popular reality show "Bigg Boss."

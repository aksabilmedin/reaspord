Who is Edward Furlong?

Edward Furlong is an American actor born on August 2, 1977, in Pasadena, California. He gained fame as a child actor and became a prominent figure in the film industry in the early 1990s. His portrayal of John Connor in "Terminator 2: Judgment Day" remains one of his most recognized roles.

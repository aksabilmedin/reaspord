What is Michael Robinson's Biography?

Michael Robinson was born on October 14, 1963, in Chicago, Illinois. He is the son of Fraser Robinson III and Marian Shields Robinson. Growing up in a modest yet supportive household, Michael, alongside his sister Michelle, was encouraged to pursue education and embrace opportunities that came their way.

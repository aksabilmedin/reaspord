What is Shaquille O'Neal's Biography?

Shaquille O'Neal, often referred to simply as Shaq, was born on March 6, 1972, in Newark, New Jersey. He displayed exceptional athletic talent from a young age, excelling in basketball during his high school years. After dominating at LSU, he was selected as the first overall pick in the 1992 NBA Draft by the Orlando Magic. Over his illustrious career, Shaq played for several teams, including the Miami Heat, Los Angeles Lakers, and Phoenix Suns, becoming a four-time NBA champion and a 15-time All-Star. Beyond basketball, he has ventured into music, acting, and business, making him a multifaceted personality in the entertainment industry.

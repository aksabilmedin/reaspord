Madison's journey into acting began with small roles in television series and short films. However, it was her audition for "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood" that marked a significant turning point in her career. Tarantino saw potential in her and cast her as a member of the Manson family, a role that would challenge her and showcase her versatility as an actress.

What Role Does Mikey Madison Play in "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood"?

In "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood," Mikey Madison plays the role of "Katie," one of the key members of the Manson family. This character is intricately woven into the film's narrative, which explores the lives of a fading actor and his stunt double in Los Angeles during the late 1960s. Madison's portrayal of Katie is both chilling and captivating, reflecting the complexity of her character's motivations and the historical context of the story.

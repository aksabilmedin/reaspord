What is Erin Darke's Biography?

Erin Darke was born on September 10, 1984, in Flint, Michigan. She grew up in a family that encouraged creativity, which led her to pursue a career in acting from a young age. What are the Key Highlights of Erin Darke's Career?

Throughout her career, Erin Darke has been involved in numerous projects that have highlighted her acting prowess. Some notable milestones include:

  • Her breakout role in the acclaimed film "Still Alice" (2014), where she starred alongside Julianne Moore.

  • Her role in the film "Love and Mercy," where she played a significant part in telling the story of Brian Wilson of the Beach Boys.

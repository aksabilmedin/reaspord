What is Jenifer Lopez's Biography?

Jenifer Lopez, often referred to as J.Lo, was born on July 24, 1969, in The Bronx, New York City. She is of Puerto Rican descent and grew up in a working-class family. Her mother, Guadalupe Rodriguez, was a schoolteacher, while her father, David Lopez, was a computer specialist. From a young age, Lopez showed an interest in the performing arts, participating in dance and music classes.

What Role Did Jenifer Lopez Play in Film?

In addition to her musical career, Jenifer Lopez has made a name for herself in Hollywood. Her breakout role came in the biopic "Selena," where she portrayed the late Tejano singer Selena Quintanilla. The performance garnered critical acclaim and established Lopez as a serious actress.

Some of her notable works include "Maid in Manhattan," "The Wedding Planner," and "Hustlers."

What is the Biography of Steve Randle?

Steve Randle is a key character in S.E. Hinton's novel "The Outsiders," published in 1967. He is depicted as a member of the Greasers, a group of working-class youths often at odds with the Socs, the affluent social group of the time. Steve is portrayed as intelligent, resourceful, and a loyal friend, often serving as a confidant to his best friend, Sodapop Curtis. His backstory reflects the struggles of growing up in a lower socio-economic environment, grappling with issues such as family dynamics and peer pressure.

What is Dwayne Johnson’s Biography?

Dwayne Douglas Johnson was born on May 2, 1972, in Hayward, California. He comes from a family with a rich wrestling background, as his father, Rocky Johnson, was a professional wrestler, and his grandfather, Peter Maivia, was also in the business. Dwayne grew up surrounded by athletes and entertainers, which laid the foundation for his future career.

After a brief stint playing college football at the University of Miami, where he won a national championship, Dwayne transitioned into professional wrestling. He debuted in the WWE in the late 1990s and quickly became a fan favorite, adopting the persona of "The Rock." His charisma and in-ring talent helped him win multiple championships and made him a household name.

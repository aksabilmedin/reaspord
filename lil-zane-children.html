Who is Lil Zane? A Brief Biography

Lil Zane, born Zane Copeland Jr. on July 21, 1982, in Atlanta, Georgia, is an American rapper and actor. He gained fame in the late 1990s and early 2000s with his debut album "Young World: The Future" and hit singles like "Callin' Me." His distinctive style and engaging lyrics quickly captured the attention of hip-hop fans worldwide. Throughout his career, Lil Zane has collaborated with various artists and remains a respected figure in the industry.
